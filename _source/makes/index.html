Explorations from Basecamp, Threadless, and other personal experiments

At Basecamp, I’m responsible for designing and building the frameworks for the new Basecamp.com marketing and Basecamp Help sites in addition to redesigning the Signal vs. Noise blog. On the product side, I recently redesigned Basecamp’s account plan selection, as well as designed Basecamp’s account pause and storage upgrades features.

Other live projects include writing my first jQuery plugin, jquery.wanker.js, a website I made to sell my bike, and Humble Pied, a video blog interviewing inspirational people. Graphically speaking…
