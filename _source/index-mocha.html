Hi, I’m Mig Reyes. I’m a graphic and web designer from Chicago working at Basecamp.

Making

I spend most of my time making and marketing Basecamp, the world’s best project management software. As a traditionally trained graphic designer, I like to experiment with other types of non-web work as well.

Writing

I’m trying to be a better writer everyday. You can read some of my favorite pieces so far: Try, try again, When culture turns to policy, and Reminder: Design is still about words.

Thinking

I believe we should practice sales and writing as much as we practice design, we should nurture our community, and I believe a few other things, too…

Speaking

My last favorite talk was one I gave at CreativeMornings Chicago. It’s four pieces of backwards advice that captures what I’ve been thinking about as we live our lives as creators.
