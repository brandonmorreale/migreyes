About the bike

This is a Schwinn Road Bike with 18 gears, built to brave the weather-worn streets of Chicago. It’s been without a rider for four years. It’s time it gets a new home. If you’re about 5'10" and like going fast, this could be the perfect ride for you.

Oh the places it’s been

Story

In the fall of 2009, I moved from the Chicago suburbs to the West Town neighborhood. Determined to ditch my car and dig into city-living, getting a brand new bike was my top priority. After doing a few months of research, my room mate and I headed to Kozy’s Cyclery to pick up what would be our very first city rides.

Every day for the next year, my trusty Schwinn took me to and from the ad agency I worked at in River West. It didn’t take too long to realize my daily commutes were very short. I decided I needed more of a beater; a less-fancy workhorse. I wanted to make a switch.

So began my search for my next bike, leaving the Schwinn without a rider. After finding a new one, it’s time for this Schwinn to find a new home. Hopefully that’s with you.
