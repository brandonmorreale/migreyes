I’m Mig Reyes, a designer at 37signals focused on crafting good things for good people.

I’m a designer from Chicago. By day, I try to do the best work I can crafting things at 37signals. By night, I teach at The Chicago Portfolio School, organize CreativeMornings Chicago, and serve as a mentor with the AIGA.

Prior to 37signals, I spent three years as an interactive designer making awesome at Threadless. I’ve also done work for other really great clients including Google, Harley-Davidson, Rand McNally, Sears and SC Johnson.

I’ve had the honor of being recognized with AIGA, Coudal, HOW, Rockport, Print, UnderConsideration and other fancy design publications. You can often find me speaking about this stuff at places such as PSFK, The School of Visual Arts, and Zappos.

Other interests include, though not limited to: pizza, beer and shots of whiskey.

Teaching

I help people put a dent in the universe, teaching Visual Design at The Starter League.

Prior, I taught design, advertising, and professional practices at The Chicago Portfolio School for three years.
